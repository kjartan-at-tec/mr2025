Transfer function from \(r(t)\) to \(y(t)\):
\[ \frac{Y(s)}{R(s)} = \frac{G(s)}{ 1+ G(s)}\]

Let \(r(t) = \sin\omega_1 t\). Then, after transients have died out,
\[ y(t) = |G_c(i\omega_1)| \sin \big( \omega_1 t + \arg G_c(i\omega_1)\big). \]
\end{frame}
